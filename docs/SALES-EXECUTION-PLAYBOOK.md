"Right. So you're probably losing ₹60-80k/month to no-shows alone.
You're already paying ₹60k/month to NOT have PatientFlow.
So the real question isn't 'Is ₹9k expensive?'
It's 'Can I afford NOT to spend ₹9k to recover ₹60k?'

One patient who stays (₹10k treatment) = your entire month fee covered.

So, let's look at your month. 
You have X appointments.
If we save just 10 from no-showing, that's ₹100k revenue.

Still think ₹9k is expensive?"

"Let's start the trial today. Risk free. 30 days. If it doesn't work, I refund you."

"OK, we have a ₹3,999 starter plan (8 hours calls support per month).
